Meet HUE!

Once upon a time I lived happily together with the minions in our colorful home. We were together everyday, wondering what color we should play with next. But then something strange happened. My friends suddenly disappeared. I waited and waited, but they did not return. But then I remembered there is but one who can restore the peaceful days to our lives. Please bring my friends back home...

The intergalactic minion finding superhero is here to the rescue! With the power of colors HUE will explore the world and use colors in a revolutionary way to find all the minions that ran away from home!

Save the minions!

These guys have run away from home in fear of the oncoming darkness, and scattered all over the world. Your mission is to get them back to their worried families, by convincing them to follow you. Some of them will be hiding deep in the forests of space, so keep your camera lenses open! Make yourself the same color as the minion, and they will follow you back home!

Explore the World!

Throughout the universe, HUE will encounter many color based puzzles of varying difficulty, where the focus is matching the color to the surrounding environment and items. Platforms will activate and lift HUE to higher grounds, doors will open only if unlocked and bridges will carry HUE over valleys if matched with the right color. Find the scared minions and lead them back to your home base!

Why did Hue find this page?

This game is a school project in the Computer Game Design course held in the Visualization Studio at The Royal Institute of Technology . The aim of this project was creating a vertical slice of one level of game play as a proof of concept during the three weeks of developement. The prototype was built with Unreal Engine 4, the 3d modeling was done in Maya. A market analylis was done showing positive outcome.
