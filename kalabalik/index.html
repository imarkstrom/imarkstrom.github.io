- Viking Band from Stockholm, Sweden -

KALABALIK is a viking band from Stockholm, Sweden, which play contemporary medieval music on a wide range of historic instruments.

Since 2009 the band has toured during the summers making appearences on a host of medieval and viking themed markets, weddings and parties.
